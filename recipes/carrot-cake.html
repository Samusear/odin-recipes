Carrot Cake

About this dish

The most moist and decadent carrot cake you will ever have! With just the right ammount of cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Cake
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut carrots
  2. Add carrots to cake
  3. Frost cake with cream cheese
  4. Enjoy!!!!
