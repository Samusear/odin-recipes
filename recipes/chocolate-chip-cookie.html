Chocolate Chip Cookies

About this dish

The gooiest inside and crispiest outside of a cookie you will ever have

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Cookies
  • hope

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Add cookies to chocolate chips
  3. space cookies evenly on baking sheet
  4. Hope the cookies don't spread to much and look gross
  5. Enjoy!!!
