About this dish

The most moist and dense Banana Bread you will ever have!

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Bread
  • Non-stick spray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Blend banana
  3. Add banana to bread
  4. Spray loaf pan
  5. Put banana bread mixture in pan
  6. Put pan in oven
  7. Enjoy!!!!
